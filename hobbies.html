Mechanical Keyboards

Building and collecting keyboards is one of my main hobby, it can be quite costy when it comes to the maintenance
including lube, brush, stab mods, case mods, etc. and the main keyboard components are the one that makes this
hobby quite expensive, we're talking about pcb brand, switches, cases, and keycaps. But this hobby I find relaxing
and can sooth my mind most especially the part where the keyboard is finally assembled.
